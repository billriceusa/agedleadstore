# Aged Lead Store Content Strategy

This repository contains comprehensive content strategy documentation and topic ideas for AgedLeadStore.com.

## Contents

### Documentation (`/docs`)
- **Content Strategy Recommendations** - Comprehensive strategy for growing high-intent lead buyer traffic
- **Content Topic Specifications** - Framework for generating consistent, SEO-optimized content topics
- **SEO Content Strategy Spec** - Technical specifications for writers and content creators
- **Brand Voice and Tone Guide** - Guidelines for maintaining consistent brand voice

### Content (`/content`)
- **50 New Topic Ideas** - High-intent buyer focused topics across priority verticals
- **Topic Ideas CSV** - Spreadsheet-ready format for content planning

## Priority Focus Areas

1. **IUL (Indexed Universal Life) Insurance Leads** - High-value niche expansion
2. **Mortgage/DSCR Leads** - Including investor-focused DSCR loan content
3. **Solar Installation Leads** - Growing market opportunities
4. **Home Improvement Leads** - Geographic and service-specific targeting

## Implementation

Topics are prioritized in three tiers:
- **Tier 1**: Immediate implementation (highest commercial intent)
- **Tier 2**: Next quarter rollout (growth categories)
- **Tier 3**: Ongoing content calendar (authority building)

All content follows established brand guidelines and excludes compliance topics (handled by legal counsel).
